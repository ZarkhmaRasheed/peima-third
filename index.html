Introduction

The Punjab Education Initiative Management Authority (PEIMA) was established in 2015 under the Punjab Education Initiative Act to transform the educational landscape of Punjab. Our mandate is to ensure equitable access to quality education for all children through innovative programs, strategic partnerships, and efficient resource management.

PEIMA works in close collaboration with the Punjab School Education Department, district administrations, and private sector partners to implement education reforms and initiatives across the province.

Our Mission

To ensure equitable access to quality education for all children in Punjab through innovative initiatives, strategic partnerships, and efficient resource management.

Our Vision

A Punjab where every child receives quality education that empowers them to become productive citizens and contribute positively to society.

Functions

  • Design and implement educational initiatives and programs
  • Manage partnerships with private sector and civil society organizations
  • Oversee the implementation of voucher schemes and school assistance programs
  • Monitor and evaluate educational outcomes and program impact
  • Develop and implement teacher training and capacity building programs
  • Coordinate with relevant government departments and international agencies

Strategy

Our strategic approach focuses on three key pillars:

  1. Access & Equity: Expanding educational opportunities for underserved communities
  2. Quality & Relevance: Enhancing teaching quality and curriculum relevance
  3. Governance & Management: Strengthening education system management and accountability

Acts & Rules

PEIMA operates under the following legislative framework:

  • Punjab Education Initiative Act, 2015
  • PEIMA Rules of Business, 2016
  • Public-Private Partnership Policy for Education
  • PEIMA Financial Regulations and Procedures

PEIMA implements a range of initiatives designed to transform education in Punjab. Each program targets specific challenges in the education sector.

Foundation Assisted Schools

Improving school infrastructure & resources

This initiative provides comprehensive support to partner schools including infrastructure development, teacher training, learning materials, and capacity building to enhance educational quality.

Financial assistance for deserving students
